TOP 3 MOVIES OF ALL TIME

Jozef's Favorite Movies

GoodFellas (1990)

A classic mafia film directed by Martin Scorsesse starring Robert Deniro, Joe Pesci & Ray Liotta. Based on the life of life of Henry Hill.

The Wolf of Wallstreet (2013)

Another insane true story directed by Martin Scorsesse, starring Leonardo Dicaprio. Based on the debauchery filled life of Jordan Belfort, a stock broker turned conman.

Toy Story (1995)

A childhood favorite of mine, a Disny Pixar film with the voices of Tim Allen and Tom Hanks as Buzz and Woody. A great heart warming family film that is fun for all.
